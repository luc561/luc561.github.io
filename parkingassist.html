Parking Assistant Using Ardunio

View the Source Code On GitHub

Utilizing an audrino controller and ultrasonic sensors; the parking assistant measures the distance of an oncoming vehicle. Based on the distance a red, yellow, or green light illuminates to director the driver when to stop. Ensuring the vehicle is parked appropriately.

Using an Arduino Uno, an ultrasonic distance sensor, and mosfets to drive a small traffic light, my brother and I designed a solution to parking in the garage of our parents' home.
