DARK (Danger Zone Alert Ranging Kit)

View the Source Code On GitHub

The main objective of D.A.R.K.(danger zone alert ranging kit) is to keep a constant line of communication between nodes and the hub. The design is aimed at teams that benefit from headcounts and/ or real time precise location of other teammates. A number of professions will benefit from D.A.R.K.(danger zone alert ranging kit). That list includes but is not limited to:

  • Construction Workers
  • Aircraft Maintenance Workers
  • Port Operators
  • Warehouse Employees
  • Factory Workers
  • Farmers

Environments are dynamic, in which, they are always changing. Utilizing UWB radio the accuracy is not affected by terrain or obstacles. While using D.A.R.K.(danger zone alert ranging kit) for its intended use, the challenges that come with a dynamic environment will not jeopardize the integrity of the product. With every change comes challenges. The uses of D.A.R.K.(danger zone alert ranging kit) has large range of uses that include but is not limited to:

  • Alerting a Team of Potential Hazards
  • Ensuring Involve Parties are Somewhere Safe
  • Real time Headcount

D.A.R.K. (Danger Zone Alert Ranging Kit) will prevent hazards from activating until everyone is safe.Workplace accidents involving heavy machinery or other hazards are still prevalent across the globe, and make headlines. With the heavy fines these incidents incur as well as the bad press it generates, D.A.R.K.'s safety function has that appeal and can be something that larger corporations would employ to keep not only their employees safe, but mitigate the costs that accidents incur.
